CONTEXT ABOUT THE PRODUCT


DreamTherapy is an iOS app centered around private dream journaling and AI-powered reflection.


The app has three primary product features:


DreamChat
A conversational reflection layer where users can explore their dreams through AI-guided dialogue.


DreamBook
A private dream journal where users save, revisit, and organize dream entries over time.


DreamCircle
A collective dream space that may allow users to explore shared dream themes or community reflection.


The app should be positioned as a reflective journaling product, not as a medical, psychological, or therapeutic service.


DreamTherapy should NOT claim to:
- diagnose mental health conditions
- replace therapy
- provide medical advice
- provide psychological treatment


AI reflections should be framed as informational and reflective only.


GENERAL WRITING PRINCIPLES


All legal text should be:


clear  
plain-language where possible  
accurate but not over-technical  
structured like real policy pages  
easy to read on mobile  
consistent with Apple privacy expectations  


Avoid:


overpromising privacy or security  
fake legal claims  
medical or therapeutic claims  
vague or evasive wording  


When uncertain about implementation details, use careful language such as:


"may collect"  
"when you choose to provide it"  
"when applicable"  


But do not make the document meaningless or vague.

PRIVACY POLICY REQUIREMENTS


Create a full Privacy Policy page with these sections:


1. Introduction


Explain what DreamTherapy is and why this policy exists.


Mention that dream content can be personal and the goal of the policy is transparency about how information may be collected and used.


2. Information We May Collect


Include realistic categories that apply to an app like DreamTherapy:


Account information such as email address if users create an account


Dream entries and journal content submitted by users


Voice recordings or audio input if the app supports voice dream capture


Usage and diagnostics information related to app performance


Device information needed for functionality or analytics


Purchase and subscription information if premium features exist


Support messages sent by users


3. How Information May Be Used


Explain possible uses such as:


providing core app functionality  
saving and organizing dream entries  
generating dream reflections using AI systems  
improving product performance and reliability  
providing reminders or notifications if enabled  
providing user support  


4. AI Processing


Explain carefully that when users submit dream content for AI reflections, that information may be processed by systems used to generate those reflections.


Make clear that AI output is informational and reflective.


Do not claim AI training behavior unless confirmed.


5. Sharing and Service Providers


Explain that DreamTherapy may rely on service providers to operate the app.


Examples may include:


hosting or infrastructure providers  
analytics services  
subscription/payment infrastructure  
AI processing providers  


Use general language unless exact providers are known.


6. Data Retention


Explain that information may be retained as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations.


7. User Choices


Include user rights such as:


accessing or updating account information  
deleting entries  
managing notifications  
requesting account deletion  
contacting support  


8. Security


Explain that reasonable safeguards are used to protect information but no system can guarantee absolute security.


9. Children’s Privacy


Explain that the app is not intended for children below the applicable age threshold.


10. Changes to This Policy


Explain that the policy may be updated and the "Last Updated" date will change.


11. Contact


Provide a placeholder support email for privacy questions.
